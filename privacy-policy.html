Privacy Policy

Last updated: 06/25/2026 — Effective for For My Personal Use (the "For My Personal Use," "we," "us")

1. Who this policy covers

This policy applies to anyone who uses the [App Name, e.g. Campus Desk] system operated by For My Personal Use, located at Pokhara, kaski, Nepal, including prospective students applying for entrance/admission, currently enrolled students, staff, coordinators, and administrators ("Users," "you").

2. What information we collect

  • Identity details — full name, date of birth, gender, photo (if uploaded), citizenship/ID number.
  • Contact details — phone number, email address, permanent and temporary address.
  • Academic details — previous school/For My Personal Use, grades/marks, program applied for, entrance exam results, attendance, results, and uploaded documents/certificates.
  • Account & login details — your assigned symbol/roll number and password (date of birth, by default).
  • Technical data — device/browser type and timestamps of when forms are submitted, used only to keep the system reliable and secure.

3. How we collect it

We collect this data directly from you when you fill in the entrance application form (including via a QR code link, which can be opened from any phone or computer), when staff enter or update your records, or when you log in to the student dashboard.

4. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored in a private database operated for For My Personal Use (currently a Google-hosted spreadsheet/backend accessible only via a private link known to the For My Personal Use, plus a local cached copy on the device you use). We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. Why we use your data

  • To process and evaluate your entrance/admission application.
  • To create and manage your student account, attendance, results, and academic records if admitted.
  • To contact you about your application or studies.
  • To allow authorized staff/coordinators/admins (only those granted access by the administrator) to view or update relevant records as part of their role.

6. Who can see your data

Only For My Personal Use staff who have been explicitly granted access to the relevant feature by an administrator can view your data. Access is role-based — for example, an accounts staff member without "Admissions" access cannot view entrance applications. Administrators have full access for the purpose of operating the system.

7. Data retention

We retain application and student records for [e.g. the duration of your studies plus 5 years], or as required by applicable education regulations, after which records may be archived or deleted.

8. Your rights

You may request to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data by contacting [contact email/phone]. We will respond within a reasonable time and make corrections where appropriate, subject to academic record-keeping requirements.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including role-based access control and restricted sync endpoints. However, no system is 100% secure, and you should not share your login password with anyone.

10. Children's data
